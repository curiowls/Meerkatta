How it works

Three steps. Zero editing.

You bring the idea. MeerKatta brings the intelligence. The tool disappears — only the result remains.

1

Record

Click the menu bar icon or press ⌘⇧R. Pick screen, camera, or both. Hit record.

2

AI Processes

MeerKatta trims silence, normalizes audio, transcribes, burns captions, and resizes — all automatically.

3

Post

Platform-ready videos with suggested titles and captions. Preview, copy, share. Done in seconds.

Features

Everything between "stop" and "post"

You handle the content. MeerKatta handles everything else.

Smart Trim

AI detects dead air, false starts, and trailing silence. Cuts to the good stuff — automatically.

Auto Captions

Local transcription in English, Chinese, and Japanese. Burned-in subtitles styled for each platform.

Platform Presets

One recording → multiple outputs. XHS (3:4), LinkedIn (16:9), Instagram (9:16), TikTok, Twitter. All at once.

Audio Fix

Broadcast-standard normalization and noise reduction. Sound professional without a studio.

Learns You

Remembers your platforms, your caption style, your preferences. Every recording gets smarter.

Local-First

All processing happens on your Mac. Your recordings never leave your device. Privacy by architecture.

The Bigger Picture

AI is powerful. But power isn't the problem.

The gap isn't capability — it's access. AI can do almost anything, if you ask right. That "if" is the product opportunity of a generation.

🎯

The Problem with General AI

General-purpose AI gives you infinite power and zero direction. Every session starts from scratch. You re-explain your style, your platforms, your preferences. The AI can edit your video brilliantly — if you write a perfect prompt. Every. Single. Time.

🦦

Specialist Faces

MeerKatta is a specialist. It doesn't do everything — it does one thing with depth. It knows video. It knows your platforms. It knows your style. The expertise is crystallized in the product so you don't have to carry it in your head.

🪄

Absorbing Complexity

Every great product absorbs the gap between what a system can do and what a human knows to ask for. Google absorbed the internet. Canva absorbed Photoshop. MeerKatta absorbs the gap between AI-powered video editing and "I just want to post this."
